The origin of the invasion

In 2019, we landed in the British Isles (Scotland, precisely) for the first time. Back then, our troops were largely not prepared, without walking boots and mostly invaded touristic spot. We did not know many things: that kilts in Braveheart are completely out of place, that Londonderry and Derry are more different things than you may know - and you better know - and that a hike to a peak just above 1000m (or 3500ft if you prefer) may seem an easy task if you were born at the foot of the Dolomites, but dear fellows, try the Mount Snowdown under the rain and clashed by the wind and let us know...
